Abuja is the capital city of Nigeria, located in the center of the country. It was officially made the capital in 1991, replacing Lagos, to promote national unity and better urban planning. Known for its modern infrastructure, wide roads, and well-organized layout, Abuja is home to important landmarks like the Aso Rock, the Nigerian National Mosque, and the National Assembly Complex.Abuja's serene environment, green landscapes, and growing economy make it one of Africa’s most planned and livable cities.
